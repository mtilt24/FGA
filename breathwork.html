Breathwork Events.

Experience the medicine of your own breath, gather, release, and remember.

Breathwork is a return to the body, a softening into what is, a reminder that the medicine has always lived inside of you.

Each session is a held space to release control, trust the breath, and let the body let go of what it has been carrying. Some events are guided solo by Becky, others are co-held with Andrea. All are welcome.

Reserve your mat, come breathe with us.
