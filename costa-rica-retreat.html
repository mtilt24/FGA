Seven nights of movement, magic, and soul-deep connection in the jungle of Nosara.

Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort Nosara, Costa Rica 7 Nights
A sacred return

Connect to Nature. Connect to Community. Connect to You.

Step away from the grind, breathe deeply, and feel alive again.

Hosted at the serene Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort, this retreat is designed for men and women ready to come home to themselves. Each day we'll weave together yoga, breathwork, cacao ceremony, ecstatic dance, sound healing, and embodied movement, alongside nature-rooted adventures such as waterfall hikes, beach time, and local excursions.

With nourishing meals, sacred ceremony, and spacious time to rest and explore, you'll be supported in slowing down and reconnecting with what truly matters. Immerse yourself in the essence of Pura Vida, the good life, the simple life, the true life, and return home grounded, open, joyful, and renewed.
